[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Tracing all your paths in the quiet of the night
Every single shadow in the soft and fading light
I've been drawing circles where your footsteps used to lay
Waiting for the moment that you look my way

[Verse 1]
Moonlight on the dashboard and a fire in the seat
Feeling every rhythm of a midnight heartbeat
Pulling up the gravel to the places we can hide
Craving for the warmth of your body by my side

[Pre-Chorus]
Every mile is burning with the memory of your touch
Never really noticed that I wanted you this much
Flicker of a candle and a whisper in the air
Reaching out my fingers just to tangle in your hair

[Chorus]
Tracing all your paths, trying to find my way to you
Lost inside the feeling and the only thing I knew
Every single road is pulling closer to your door
I don't want to wander in the distance anymore

[Post-Chorus]
Tracing every line, following the track
Tracing all your paths, never looking back

[Verse 2]
Rain against the window of a quiet little place
Watching every shadow, tracing lines upon your face
Looking at the embers burning steady in the dark
Waiting for a touch that will ignite a heavy spark

[Pre-Chorus 2]
Every mile is burning with the memory of your touch

Reaching out my fingers just to tangle in your hair

[Chorus]
Tracing all your paths, trying to find my way to you
Lost inside the feeling and the only thing I knew

[Post-Chorus 2]
Tracing every line, following the track
Tracing all your paths, never looking back

[Bridge]
Following the river till it leads right into town
Never gonna stop until my walls are coming down
Even when the shadows try to keep us far apart
Saeed leaves a promise in the center of your heart

[Spoken]
(I just want to be where you are)
(Following the rhythm of the dark)

[Final Chorus]
Tracing all your paths, trying to find my way to you
